### Areas for Improvement and Future Development

Reflecting objectively on my self-assessment, I recognize several key areas where I need to focus my efforts to grow:
- **Enhancing Technical Knowledge & Cloud Skills (Criteria 1 & 10):** My practical expertise in AWS and Cloud Engineering is still basic. When resolving complex network routing issues in VPCs, configuring granular IAM Policies, or fixing API Gateway routing errors, I often spend excessive time troubleshooting. I need to improve my ability to analyze CloudWatch logs and study optimal architectural design patterns.
- **Boosting Proactiveness & Contribution (Criteria 3 & 11):** Throughout the `GlobalMart` project, I was sometimes passive, waiting for direct instructions or tasks from my mentor or team members instead of proposing solutions. I need to be more proactive in driving pipeline design (CI/CD) and managing lab resources (cleaning up idle EBS volumes or NAT Gateways).
- **Improving Team Collaboration (Criteria 8):** Coordination within the team was occasionally disconnected, with members working in silos on their frontend/backend modules. I need to communicate progress more frequently, engage in design reviews, and assist teammates promptly to build a more integrated workflow.
